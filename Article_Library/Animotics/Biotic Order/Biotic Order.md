## Core Statistics

**Biotic** is the **parent order** of the **Terric Domain**, the **Geotic Realm**, and the **Gaean Province**.  All members of those narrower types are, by definition, **Biotic**.    

About $1$ in $9.6$ ($0.1037$%) duramons are biotic worlds, and within the biotic order: 
    - About $1.7$% of Biotics are in the broadest [[Terric Domain]] (parahabitable)        
    - The [[Geotic Realm]] (human-habitables) account for about $0.34$% of all Biotics (habitable)        
    - The [[Gaean Province]] (human-hospitable worlds) account for about $0.17$% of all Biotics (about half of all Geotics)        
    - The [[Kybelic Domain]] is the "superhabitable" counterpart of the Terric Domain.
	    - About $\approx 1.2$% of all Biotics qualify as **Kybelic** (parahabitable for humans; highly animegic to carboxyhydroxyl life), a slightly smaller percentage than the Terric Domain.
        

## <u>Important Note</u>

**The Biotic phase space defines the _physical_ conditions under which COH-amegic life is _possible_, not guaranteed.**  A duramon falls within the Biotic Order if its mass, radius, density, surface gravity, and escape velocity occupy the narrow region of parameter space in which **COH solvents, COH chemistry, and surface-accessible metabolisms could, in principle, arise and persist.**

Worlds _outside_ the Biotic phase space are **physically unlikely** to host COH life, because they cannot maintain the structural or energetic conditions required for liquid-phase biochemistry.

Worlds _inside_ the Biotic phase space are not necessarily COH-amegic â€” many will lack oceans, atmospheres, or stable climates. They simply possess the **structural eligibility** for such systems to exist.

The narrower Animotic classes ([[Terric Domain|Terric]] â†’ [[Geotic Realm|Geotic]] â†’ [[Gaean Province|Gaean]]) represent progressively smaller regions of this same phase space where a worldâ€™s physical properties are **increasingly well-tuned** for COH-amegicity. These classes describe **potential**, not environmental actuality; the realization of that potential is governed by Milieutic factors such as atmosphere, hydrosphere, stellar flux, and planetary history.

Being Biotic does not guarantee the presence of COH life; it only guarantees the _possibility_ of COH life given appropriate environmental conditions and planetary history.
